Chilli Garlic Prawn Noodles

Ready in 30 minutes,this stir-fried prawn,egg and noodles dish is perfect for a midweek meal...
Spicy and lip smacking -noodles cooked with chillies,garlic and prawns.quick and easy to make chilli Garlic prawns Noodles is a great lunch box dish or for dinner also.Chilli Garlic Prawns Noodles is a Indo Chinese dish ..it is so easy to prepare and also tastes good.
You can even use Whole wheat noodles to make it healthier.
Pop in the prawns and stir-fry for a couple of mins until they have just turned pink.
